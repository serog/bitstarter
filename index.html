Advantages

No chemicals, including DEET, so it's safe for children. It's also very cooling, so it's pleasant for people who overheat easily. Skeeter Armor will be available in various styles including Modern Minimalist, Goth, D&D Dork, and Wonder Woman.

How it works

Small fans push air through channels within the gauntlet and is expelled in a consistent stream along the skin. Mosquitos are not strong fliers in wind and are easily pushed away with a breeze.
